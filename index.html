What?

At our weekly meetups your kids (aged 8 - 13) will develop mad computer programming skills by designing, building and coding robots to solve navigational challenges.

Why?

Robotics is exploding as a tool to get kids engaged in STEM and on the road to developing mad computer programming skills. Bring your kids (aged 8 - 13) to our weekly meetups where we will explore the challenges of robotic navigation. Your kids will get hands-on experience with using mathematics and programming to face down engineering challenges.
